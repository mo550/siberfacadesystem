design assist

When working on a construction project with complex building geometries or high-performance materials, getting input from the experts can reduce the time and costs to design and build a project.

We have assisted some of the most cutting-edge developers in the early stages of a project's development to provide insights into the project's cost and constructability, identifying risks and practical risks.

design assessment

Input from industry experts during the pre-construction process can help to identify design issues that would not have been evident until a project is in the construction phase.

We look at potential installation issues, structure conflicts, and means of attachment and steer the design in a direction that allows those types of issues to be addressed early on, saving time and money during the construction stage. This can also help reduce the number of design related RFIs and change orders.
