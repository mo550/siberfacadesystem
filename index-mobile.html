CO2 REDUCTION

Our products provide a solution to energy usage and help drop CO2 by reducing excess heating and cooling. When it comes to sustainable responsibility, we take great care in aligning our values with environmentally sound manufacturing companies, utilize recycled products, ensure waste reduction on site, and do our due diligence in auditing potential Partners.

EFFECTIVELY MINIMIZED EMISSIONS

Researchers find that 23% of carbon dioxide emissions from global economic activities can be attributed to the construction sector. It's now more important than ever for our industry to be leading the way on climate action. Siber focuses on reducing the operational energy demand in buildings with smart facade structures that ensure superior thermal performance.
